This is your opponent, Earthling. I have heard every word  you have said.  I'm weary of the chase. Wait for me. I shall be  merciful and quick.  You were intruding! You established an outpost in our space.  We destroyed invaders, as I shall destroy you!  Captain, let us be reasonable.  You have lost. Admit it to yourself. Stop  running.  (Now the sulphur goes into the cannon, and finally he finds a vein on  coal.)  SPOCK; Coal.  (Now Kirk settles down to empty the bamboo, crush the coal, make a hole  in the bamboo for a fuse and then mix his ingredients a little more  precisely.)  I sent him back to his ship. If you like, I shall destroy him for  you.
